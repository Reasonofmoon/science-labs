Building the Periodic Table Experiment

Arrange Elements and Discover Periodic Trends

Explore the logic behind Mendeleev's revolutionary organization of the elements.

Steps:

  1. Study the properties of various elements provided.
  2. Arrange the elements based on their atomic weights and chemical properties.
  3. Identify patterns and trends in the arrangement.
  4. Predict properties of unknown or undiscovered elements based on your arrangement.

Experience the process of creating one of the most important tools in chemistry!
