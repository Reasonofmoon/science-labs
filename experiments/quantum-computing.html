Quantum Computing Basics Experiment

Explore Superposition and Entanglement

Dive into the fascinating world of quantum computing and its fundamental principles.

Steps:

  1. Use a quantum simulator to create and manipulate qubits.
  2. Explore the concept of superposition by putting qubits in multiple states simultaneously.
  3. Demonstrate quantum entanglement by creating correlated qubit pairs.
  4. Implement simple quantum algorithms and compare them with classical counterparts.

Experience the potential future of computing and information processing!
