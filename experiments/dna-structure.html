Unraveling DNA's Structure Experiment

Decode the Blueprint of Life

Follow in the footsteps of Watson, Crick, and Franklin to discover the structure of DNA.

Steps:

  1. Examine X-ray crystallography images of DNA molecules.
  2. Build physical models of possible DNA structures using provided materials.
  3. Analyze how different structures might explain DNA's ability to store and replicate genetic information.
  4. Compare your models with the actual double helix structure of DNA.

Experience the process of one of the most significant scientific discoveries of the 20th century!
