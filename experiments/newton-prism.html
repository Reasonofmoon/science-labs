Newton's Prism Experiment

Discover the Nature of Light and Color

Explore how white light is composed of different colors using a prism.

Steps:

  1. Set up a prism in a dark room with a single beam of white light.
  2. Observe the spectrum of colors produced when light passes through the prism.
  3. Use a second prism to recombine the colors back into white light.
  4. Experiment with different colored lights and observe their spectra.

Witness the fundamental nature of light as discovered by Sir Isaac Newton!
