Faraday's Electromagnetic Discoveries Experiment

Explore the Relationship Between Electricity and Magnetism

Recreate Faraday's groundbreaking experiments in electromagnetism.

Steps:

  1. Create a simple electromagnet using a nail, wire, and battery.
  2. Demonstrate electromagnetic induction using a magnet and a coil of wire.
  3. Build a simple electric motor using magnets, wire, and a battery.
  4. Observe how changing magnetic fields can induce electric currents.

Experience the fundamental principles that led to the development of modern electrical technology!
