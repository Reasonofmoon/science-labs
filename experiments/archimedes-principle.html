Archimedes' Principle Experiment

Explore Buoyancy and Fluid Displacement

In this experiment, we will explore Archimedes' principle of buoyancy and fluid displacement.

Steps:

  1. Fill a container with water and mark the water level.
  2. Carefully place an object in the water and observe the new water level.
  3. Calculate the volume of water displaced.
  4. Measure the weight of the object and compare it to the weight of the displaced water.

Observe how the buoyant force is equal to the weight of the displaced fluid!
