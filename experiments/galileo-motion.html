Galileo's Motion Studies Experiment

Investigate Laws of Motion

Recreate Galileo's famous experiments with inclined planes and pendulums.

Steps:

  1. Set up an inclined plane with a smooth surface.
  2. Roll balls of different masses down the plane and measure their acceleration.
  3. Create pendulums of different lengths and observe their periods.
  4. Record and analyze your observations to discover the laws of motion.

Experience how Galileo challenged Aristotelian physics and laid the groundwork for Newton's laws!
